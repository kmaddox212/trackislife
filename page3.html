Events

Sprint

  • 60M

    • Primarily an event for indoor tracks. This is the shortest race from the collegiate and pro levels.

    World Record "WR"


    Men's: 6.39 set in 1998 by Maurice Greene (USA)
    Women's: 6.92 set in 1993 by Irina Privalova (RUS)

  • 100M

    • The shortest of outdoor sprints. Also one of the most anticipated as this is where we determine the fastest men and women in the world. A position that has been dominated by Jamaica in the past unfortunately; but USA isn't too far behind.

    World Records "WR"


    Men's: 9.58 set in 2009 by Usain Bolt (JAM)
    Women's: 10.49 set in 1988 by Florence Griffith-Joyner, "FloJo" (USA)

  • 200M

    • This is one race that is near and dear to me as it is one that I ran primarily. Just half the distance of the track, this event is physically breath taking as you are constantly upshifting gears.

    World Record "WR"


    Men's: 19.19 set in 2009 by Usain Bolt (JAM)
    Women's: 21.34 set in 1988 by Florence Griffith-Joyner (USA)
